IDM Showcase

Fandango.com is an online ticketing company that sells movie tickets. As a project, I redesigned their mobile application to include a more user-friendly experience.

Process

Vision

New York University's Integrated Digital Media (IDM) department at their Tandon School of Engineering exhibits a yearly showcase for students to display their work. These pieces range from graphic design showpieces to VR activities.
I designed the user interface of the website students will access to get tickets or sign up to share personal projects. Together with the IDM Showcase committee, I created the site that will launch in March.
